Welcome To Kiwano

We want to establish ourselves as the leader of an ethical approach that complies with regulations. Transparency, respect for privacy, and interest in our community are our central values. Because Kiwano is, above all, a community service, which takes the form of a tree: the roots, embodied by Kiwano-Strategy, will constitute the private part of Kiwano, in search of profit to be able to bring growth to the Kiwano tree. The trunk will take shape in Kiwano-Community, and will constitute the public part and the user experience.

Be part of A Community

By the community, for the community: we provide a free and open-source software for portfolio management. This software will be managed from a participative social network, following a decentralized governance model. Moreover, the goal for our community is to reach financial freedom. To accomplish this, education in finance is a key point of our approach, and we want to concentrate on collective intelligence and individual responsibility, the only bulwark against excesses and abuses.
