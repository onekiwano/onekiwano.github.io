Dollar Cost Average (DCA)

The term "DCA" stands for Dollar Cost Average, and it is the safest way to invest in a crypto-asset. The DCA invests a sum of money over a period and splits this quantity by the time interval chosen by the user, for example, by investing 25 USD each week.

In the example below, we backtested the DCA for five years of Bitcoin prices:

SmartDCA

We improved the concept of DCA by having an adaptive version called the SmartDCA. This approach is solely based on maths but is rather simple to understand; the algo invests more when the price goes down and less when the price goes up.

In the same market condition, we backtested the SmartDCA, which performs 44% better!

We are extremely confident our algorithm can make you earn money, as we devised a mathematical proof that in all market conditions, it performs better and, in the worst-case scenario, as well as the DCA.
